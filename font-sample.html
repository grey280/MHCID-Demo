Spooky

This font feels like Tolkein, and elves.

But not the friendly kind; these are the old British elves, who never lie... but that doesn't mean they're telling the truth.

"Come dance with us," they say, "come away to our woods."

What they don't say is that you may never come home... or perhaps you will, and find the world moved on without you.

Trying too hard.

This font says "hubris."

It's the font that comes from someone wanting to redeem Comic Sans.

It's trying too hard to be silly and fun, but also... serious enough to be seriously used.
